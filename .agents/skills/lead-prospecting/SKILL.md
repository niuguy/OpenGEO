# Lead Prospecting

Use this skill for reviewed, non-automated local prospect workflows.

## Workflow

1. Define category, location, and prospect limit.
2. Use official data sources where available, such as Google Places API.
3. Crawl only public business websites and contact pages.
4. Collect emails, phone hints, forms, and audit notes.
5. Do not submit forms or send outreach automatically.
6. Prioritize prospects by audit relevance and likely value.

## Output

Return:

- prospect list
- contact availability
- website audit observations
- suggested manual outreach angle
