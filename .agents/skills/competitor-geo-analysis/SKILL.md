# Competitor GEO Analysis

Use this skill when comparing AI visibility across local competitors.

## Workflow

1. Identify the target business and competitors.
2. Review completed prompt runs across providers.
3. Compare recommendation frequency and observed rank.
4. Find prompts where competitors displace the target.
5. Inspect reasons, semantic attributes, and reference signals attached to competitor mentions.
6. Identify practical experiments for the target business.

## Output

Return:

- competitor ranking summary
- displacement examples
- likely reference-signal advantages
- content/citation opportunities
- prompts to monitor next
