# AI Visibility Audit

Use this skill when a user wants to understand how a business appears in AI recommendation answers.

## Workflow

1. Locate the OpenGEO business record.
2. Review active prompts and provider configuration.
3. Run or inspect recent prompt samples.
4. Compare target appearances, ranks, recommendation rate, share of voice, and competitor displacement.
5. Review semantic attributes and reference signals mentioned by models.
6. Separate observed answer behavior from unverified causation.
7. Produce a concise client-ready interpretation.

## Output

Return:

- visibility summary
- strongest prompts
- weakest prompts
- competitor threats
- reference signal patterns
- recommended content or citation experiments
